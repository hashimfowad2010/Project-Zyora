2. Governmental Interview

What we did: We asked the government officials to also keep a check and balance on not only just the restaurants but also on the local stalls.

Findings

We found how government of Pakistsn tackles this issue. the representative told that first they issue warnings to unsustainable food processors, then they charge them heavy fines and finally they imprison themif the activity continues.
